Glitching Lights

Date: 2020

Dimension:N/A

Mediums: Touch Desgin, HD color video, 0"2"56, and Adobe Premiere Pro

Description: This piece was originally an exercise for one of my classes to explore with Touch Design. However, it transformed into an interesting piece where I glitched clips that I had filmed of different sources of light to create a "symphony of light".
