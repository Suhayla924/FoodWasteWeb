Flower Crown

Date:2017

Dimension:500x600px

Mediums: Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop

Description: The piece was created by using a photo filter on top of a photo to render these "color blocks" in Adobe Illustrator. After that, we then simplified the colors to create more solid shapes to create the portrait and was colored using the Live Paint tool.
